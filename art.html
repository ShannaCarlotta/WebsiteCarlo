Here are some artists I personally really like!

„Flossywaters“ is a young British illustrator. In the past few years she has already completed her Bachelor's degree in Graphic Arts and Illustrations at Southampton University and is now studying at the Royal College of Art (one of the world's best design schools). There she will do her Masters in Visual Communications. Florence, as mentioned above, is a great inspiration to me. Just last year she designed her first book, a picture book for adults, called “The Adventure of Doris Florence Valentine”, sold clothes with her designs, posters, prints and much more. In my opinion, she does a truly admirable job expressing her unique style and talent for illustration.


Flossy's IG: flossywaters



„Optik“ is an artist from Cologne, who is mainly known for his colorful and rather chaotic illustrations. He has worked with some big brands like Doc Martens, Ford, Obi, Asics and Redbull and designed album covers for BHZ, Lugatti and co. He shares his art on Tik Tok and Instagram and runs an online store with posters, sweaters, lighters and more. He is also committed to promoting young talent, offers drawing courses and posts tutorials on social media. In my opinion, he has a very exciting style and uses his reach wisely to do something good.


Optik's IG: flossywaters
